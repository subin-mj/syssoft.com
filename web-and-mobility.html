MOBILITY

Would you like to showcase your web presence over mobile? Would you like to develop a mobile application? Sysgsoft is your answer. At Sysgsoft, our team of developers and designers are masters in developing native applications. Having built mobility solutions for different organizations across the globe, we provide a hub for all native mobile technologies. Our team of software architects, PMs, and business analysts sit together to build a framework of the application and identify its features, usability, monetization strategy and all other key components. Our team then prepares a wireframe to visualize your future application which once approved moves to the UI team to develop the user interface. Once the design is completed and approved, the app coding begins and the app is finally tested on many screens including all major Android & IOS phones with different OS. Our team has expertise in building mobile web applications using HTML5, Jquery Mobile & Sencha Touch along with developing applications on all the leading cross-mobile platforms including PhoneGap, Titanium Appcelerator and RhoMobile.
